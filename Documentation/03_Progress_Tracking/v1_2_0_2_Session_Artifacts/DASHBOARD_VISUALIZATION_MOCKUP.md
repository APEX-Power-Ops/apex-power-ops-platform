## 💡 WHAT THIS SHOWS YOU

### **All These Views Come From Your Data Fields:**

**Executive Dashboard** uses:
- Projects.Percent_Complete
- Projects.Schedule_Performance_Index
- Projects.Cost_Performance_Index
- Projects.Acceptable_Count / Total_Apparatus_Count
- Projects.Project_Target_Completion
- Projects.Forecast_Completion_Date

**Scope Schedule** uses:
- Scope.NETA_Standard (Testing_Standard)
- Scope.Planned_Start_Date
- Scope.Actual_Start_Date
- Scope.Target_Completion_Date
- Scope.Days_To_Target

**Task Progress** uses:
- Tasks.Percent_Complete
- Tasks.Total_Apparatus_Count
- Tasks.Completed_Apparatus_Count
- Tasks.Total_Actual_Hours
- Tasks.Pass_Rate_Percentage

**Apparatus Detail** uses:
- Apparatus.Tag_Number
- Apparatus.Apparatus_Type
- Apparatus.Location
- Apparatus.Completion_Status
- Apparatus.Apparatus_Assessment ← **YOU'RE ADDING THIS NOW**
- Apparatus.Actual_Hours

### **The Power of Good Data Architecture:**

With the fields you're adding, you can automatically generate:
- ✅ Executive dashboards (Power BI)
- ✅ Field technician mobile apps (Canvas)
- ✅ Client status reports (Power Automate + Word templates)
- ✅ Schedule alerts (Power Automate flows)
- ✅ Quality notifications (when Non-Serviceable found)
- ✅ Performance analytics (hours/item, items/day)

**All without manual data entry or Excel calculations!**

---

## 🎯 THIS IS WHY YOUR ARCHITECTURE MATTERS

**Without proper fields:**
- PMs manually create these reports (2 hours each)
- Data gets stale (weekly updates only)
- Errors creep in (copy/paste mistakes)
- No real-time alerts (find out too late)
- Can't drill down (high-level only)

**With your Dataverse solution:**
- Reports auto-generate from live data
- Always current (updates every few minutes)
- Zero manual calculation errors
- Instant alerts on issues
- Full drill-down capability (Project → Scope → Task → Apparatus)
